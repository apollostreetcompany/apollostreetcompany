## Design Context

### Users
- Japanese companies looking to enter the US market
- US companies looking to enter the Japanese market
- Decision-makers evaluating cross-border investment and execution partners

### Brand Personality
Sophisticated, connected. A premium venture studio and search fund — not a creative agency, not a consultancy. Operator-investors who build and execute, not just advise.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Dark, cinematic, confident — the existing direction is right, just needs consistency
- **References**: Premium venture studio + search fund aesthetic
- **Anti-references**: Creative agency portfolio (the Brabus template DNA), generic consulting, overly corporate
- **Theme**: Dark mode primary, light content sections for contrast
- **Colors**: Navy (#002d62) is the anchor. Red (#e8293b) as accent. Gold (#f6d894) is optional — present in slide imagery but not a required brand color.

### Design Principles
1. **Operator credibility over agency flash** — every element should signal "we build and invest" not "we design websites"
2. **Restraint over spectacle** — the template has theatrics (preloader, autoplay audio, animated equalizer) that don't serve the brand; strip what doesn't earn its place
3. **Bilingual parity** — EN and JP content must feel equally intentional, not afterthought translations
4. **Consistency over novelty** — fix the design system (fonts, colors, spacing) before adding features
